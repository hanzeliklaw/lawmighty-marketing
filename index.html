Where Lawmighty wins

  • Flat per-firm pricing. Add staff without adding cost. A 5-person firm saves roughly $4,000–$5,000 a year.
  • AI built into the workflow. Discovery drafting and extraction are core features, not a premium upsell.
  • Real family-law substance. TN child support calculator, parenting plan generator, and juvenile forms ship working.
  • No tier hostage-taking. Trust accounting, client portal, and document generation are all in the base price.

Where we're still building

  • Native mobile apps. Fully responsive on every device today; dedicated iOS and Android apps are on the roadmap.
  • Built-in payments. LawPay integration is in progress so clients can pay straight from the portal.
  • Integration ecosystem. The community template marketplace is our long-game answer to incumbent app stores.
  • e-Signature. Native signing is planned to round out the document workflow.
